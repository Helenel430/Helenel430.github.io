In this project, our lab develops a dataset consisting of individually recorded, freely-moving female and male mice that were exposed to chronic non-discriminatory social defeat stress. We create an automated pipeline that processes videos of freely moving mice. It outputs clear video frames showing the mouse side profile, which will be used by other machine learning models to score stress in the future.
